KNF
Concept: KNF fermenting is a natural way to provide consintrated minerals broken down by microrganizims with the help of brown sugar. You dont have to spend an arm and a leg at the local grow store for fertilizer. Example: If your looking to boost your harvest volume, add wood ash and dried/crushed egg shells to your top soil. Also add potassium by creating a bannana KNF receipt 
Things need:
paper towel 
rubber band
glass jar
brown sugar
unpilled bannanas

Steps:
1. Add chopped bannanas in the jar
2. Add brown sugar on top
3. Hand press till compressed 
4. Add brown sugar at the top
5. Place the paper on top and seal it with a rubberband
6. Set for a week and then add a cap size amount to handsprayer or watering can

Conclusion:
You can switch up the organic material depending if your in the flowering or budding phase.
